SightSage Marketing Representative Application

OVERVIEW

Are you looking for a flexible part-time job while going to school where you can make excellent compensation, gain work experience, make a positive impact in people’s lives, help fellow students excel, and meet new friends all at once?

Welcome to the life of a Marketing Representative at SightSage. You’ll create your own work schedule, work will take place on-campus and at your home and you’ll be tasked with promoting SightSage’s best in class vision wellness products to fellow students at your college/university both in person and online.

SightSage Foods and Nutrition Inc. is a Canadian company that manufactures and distributes food-based products designed to support eyesight and overall wellness.

SightSage brings the goodness of nature and contemporary nutrition science together to create an innovative brand of functional foods and nutrition products. Product development is led by SightSage founder, Dr. Weidong Yu, a Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine renowned for his successful treatment of retinal eye disorders.

Crafted by over 30 years of traditional Chinese medicine expertise and natural nutrition, our products are specifically designed to improve eye comfort and help you achieve more.

SightSage products were formulated at Wellspring Clinic in Vancouver, Canada. At the clinic, Kathy Zhang, wife of Dr. Weidong Yu and fellow practitioner, runs the @kathyhealthtips brand which is closely associated with SightSage. Kathy has amassed 1.4 million followers on TikTok, and over 130 k followers on Instagram. While working at SightSage, you will have the opportunity to collaborate with @kathyhealthtips.
